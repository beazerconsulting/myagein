## Full description (max 4000 chars)
How old are you... really?

My Age In turns your birthdate into a collection of surprising, share-worthy
life milestones. Instead of plain years, discover the exact date and time you
reach big round numbers — like 1 billion seconds, 10,000 days, or 1 million
hours old.

Just enter your birthday and instantly see:

• MILESTONE COUNTDOWNS — The precise moment you hit fun numeric milestones
  across seconds, minutes, hours, days, weeks, and months. Including playful
  novelty numbers like 1,234 days and 12,345 days old.

• YOUR AGE, EVERY WAY — See your current age measured in every unit of time at
  once, updating live to the second.

• AT YOUR AGE IN HISTORY — Discover the remarkable people from ancient and
  medieval history who were exactly your age, and what they were doing.

• AGE ACROSS THE SOLAR SYSTEM — Find out how old you'd be on Mars, Jupiter,
  and every other planet.

• ANIMAL LIFETIMES — Compare your lifespan to the animal kingdom.

• BEAUTIFUL EXPORTS — Save a sleek shareable infographic of your milestones,
  or export the full breakdown to a spreadsheet.

Clean, fast, and completely private — your birthdate never leaves your phone.
No account required. No ads.

Whether you're planning a unique birthday surprise, looking for a fun fact, or
just curious about the numbers behind your life, My Age In gives you a fresh
perspective on the time you've lived.

Download now and find your next milestone.
